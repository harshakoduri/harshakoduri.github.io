Working at PCT, a Caladrius Company

Join our team of scientific, technological and operational experts who are working at the leading edge of cellular therapeutics—a revolutionary area of medicine with the potential to reduce suffering and cure chronic disease. PCT is at the forefront of this developing industry, and includes leaders in innovation and cGMP manufacturing using their combined expertise to advance client products with commercialization in mind.  

 

Employee Career Path Development Program

When our employees are at their best, PCT is at its best. In order for us to serve our clients and reach the ambitious goals we set for our business, we need to develop ourselves, both individually and as a team.

PCT strives to offer its employees an environment in which they are challenged, educated, rewarded, satisfied and excited to be part of a world-class team that is at the cutting edge of the regenerative medicine industry. Accordingly, a critical element of our culture is to help our employees reach their professional potential.

Whether each employee’s position at PCT is in leadership, quality, development, or production and operations, we provide our employees with a detailed Success Profile, Competency Model, and Career Path, developed with input from team members who have succeeded at PCT. This program describes the essential elements of success in these roles, including behaviors, attitudes and skills, as well as experiences and developmental activities that can help you get there. Through this development program as well we a competitive benefits package, we encourage employee retention, providing additional stability to long-term client engagements.
